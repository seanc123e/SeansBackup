Must Read Sci-Fi Books for Developers

  • The Time Machine by H. G. Wells (1895)
  • War of the Worlds by H. G. Wells (1898)
  • The Robot Series by Isaac Asimov (1950)
  • The Foundation Series by Isaac Asimov (1951)
  • Starship Troopers by Robert Heinlein (1959)
  • Stranger in a Strange Land by Robert Heinlein (1961)
This list of the most important science fiction books for software developers was voted on by thousands of devoted science fiction readers. When you hear Sci-Fi, do your normally think of H.G. Wells, Isaac Asimov, Jules Verne, Arthur C. Clarke, or Robert Heinlein? Or do some more recent authors come to mind? Click on any title below to go to its Wikipedia page if you want to know more.
  • Dune Saga by Frank Herbert (1965)
  • The The Moon Is a Harsh Mistress by Robert Heinlein (1966)
  • 2001: A Space Odyssey by Arthur C. Clarke (1968)
  • The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy by Douglas Adams (1979)
  • Neuromancer by William Gibson (1984)
  • The Ender Saga by Orson Scott Card (1985)
  • Hyperion by Dan Simmons (1989)
  • Ready Player One by Ernest Cline (2011)
